Burner Won't Ignite

LG gas cooktop ignition repair diagnoses burners that won't light when the knob is turned. Gas cooktop ignition requires the igniter to spark while gas flows to the burner—failure of either component prevents ignition. Common causes include wet igniters from boilovers, clogged burner ports, failed spark modules, and gas valve problems. Our technicians systematically diagnose ignition failures throughout Hackensack, Paramus, and Bergen County gas cooktop installations.

Weak Flame

LG gas cooktop weak flame repair addresses burners producing insufficient heat for cooking. Food debris and grease buildup commonly restrict gas flow over time. Our technicians clean burner assemblies and adjust gas flow throughout Teaneck, Fort Lee, and Bergen County.

Clicking But No Ignition

LG gas cooktop clicking repair fixes burners where the igniter clicks repeatedly but flame doesn't establish. Our technicians identify the specific failure throughout Ridgewood, Englewood, and Bergen County.
