Factory Certified LG Washer Repair

Front Load Washer Repair

Benefits of Front Load Washers:

  • Energy and water efficient
  • Gentler on clothes, less wear and tear
  • Larger capacity for bulky items
  • Faster spin cycles, shorter drying times
  • Stackable to save space
  • Quieter operation

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Door seal mold and mildew buildup
  • Bearing failure causing loud noises
  • Door latch problems preventing start
  • Drain pump clogs from debris
  • UE error from unbalanced loads
  • Water leaks from door gasket

Top Load Washer Repair

Benefits of Top Load Washers:

  • Easier to load and unload
  • No need to bend over
  • Faster wash cycles
  • Add forgotten items mid-cycle
  • Lower upfront cost
  • Less maintenance required

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Agitator not moving or broken
  • Lid switch failure preventing operation
  • Transmission issues causing no spin
  • Water pump failures and drainage problems
  • Timer or control board malfunctions
  • Basket not spinning due to clutch wear

Commercial Washer Repair

Benefits of Commercial Washers:

  • Heavy-duty construction
  • High capacity for large loads
  • Designed for frequent use
  • Faster cycle times
  • Energy and water efficient
  • Longer lifespan

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Coin mechanism failures in laundromats
  • Frequent bearing replacements from heavy use
  • Motor overheating from constant operation
  • Suspension system wear from large loads
  • Control board failures from power surges
  • Water valve issues from hard water deposits

Residential Washer Repair

Benefits of Residential Washers:

  • Quiet operation
  • Energy Star rated models
  • Customizable wash cycles
  • Compact and space-saving
  • Smart features and connectivity
  • Affordable and reliable

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Wi-Fi connectivity problems in smart models
  • Sensor errors affecting wash cycles
  • Door lock malfunctions preventing operation
  • Detergent dispenser clogs and leaks
  • Error codes from software glitches
  • Drain hose kinks causing water backup

Portable Washer Repair

Benefits of Portable Washers:

  • No permanent installation needed
  • Perfect for small spaces
  • Easy to move and store
  • Lower water and energy use
  • Affordable and convenient
  • Great for apartments and RVs

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Hose connection leaks from frequent setup
  • Small motor burnout from overuse
  • Wheels breaking from constant movement
  • Limited wash power for heavy fabrics
  • Adapter fitting failures at faucet
  • Drain hose placement causing flooding

Washer/Dryer Combo Repair

Benefits of Washer/Dryer Combos:

  • Space-saving all-in-one unit
  • No need to transfer clothes
  • Energy and water efficient
  • Convenient for small homes
  • Ventless options available
  • Modern and compact design

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Long cycle times (3-4 hours total)
  • Drying function less effective than standalone
  • Condensation drain clogs affecting drying
  • Complex repairs requiring specialized parts
  • Moisture sensor failures in dry cycle
  • Heating element burnout in ventless models

Stackable Washer Repair

Benefits of Stackable Washers:

  • Maximizes vertical space
  • Ideal for small laundry rooms
  • Easy to access both units
  • Can be paired with matching dryer
  • Modern and sleek appearance
  • Efficient use of space

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Stacking kit failure or instability
  • Excessive vibration during spin cycle
  • Difficulty accessing top unit controls
  • Misalignment causing operational issues
  • Dryer lint buildup affecting both units
  • Water leaks from connection points

Compact Washer Repair

Benefits of Compact Washers:

  • Perfect for apartments and small spaces
  • 24-inch width fits tight areas
  • Energy and water efficient
  • Portable or built-in options
  • Ideal for single or couple households
  • Quiet operation for small living spaces

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Overloading causing poor wash results
  • Drain pump clogs from small capacity
  • Door seal leaks due to compact design
  • Limited spin speed affecting drying time
  • Control panel malfunctions
  • Inlet valve failures from hard water

High-Efficiency (HE) Washer Repair

Benefits of High-Efficiency Washers:

  • Uses 20-66% less water than standard
  • Energy Star certified models
  • Reduced utility bills over time
  • Gentler on fabrics with tumble action
  • Larger capacity for bigger loads
  • Advanced wash cycles and features

Common Issues & Solutions:

  • Requires HE detergent to prevent sudsing
  • Mold and mildew from low water usage
  • Long wash cycles (60-90 minutes)
  • Door seal cleaning maintenance required
  • Error codes from sensor sensitivity
  • Bearing failures from heavy loads
