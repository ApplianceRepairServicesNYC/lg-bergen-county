Front Load Washer Expertise

LG front load washer repair demands specialized knowledge of horizontal-axis drum systems and their unique mechanical requirements. Our technicians expertly diagnose door boot seal deterioration that causes water leaks, bearing assemblies that produce grinding noises during high-speed spins, and spider arm failures resulting from detergent buildup corrosion. We address door latch mechanisms preventing cycle initiation, drain pump failures triggering error codes, and shock absorber degradation causing excessive cabinet movement. Front-loading machines require precise leveling and load balancing—our factory-trained specialists ensure whisper-quiet operation and optimal cleaning performance using manufacturer-approved replacement components.

Top Load Washer Solutions

LG top load washer repair encompasses both conventional agitator designs and modern high-efficiency impeller systems. Our expertise covers lid switch malfunctions that interrupt cycles, transmission failures affecting agitation and spin functions, and water inlet valve problems causing fill irregularities. We remedy agitator coupling deterioration, worn drive belts causing slippage, and suspension system failures leading to off-balance conditions. From tub bearing replacements to motor coupler repairs and electronic control diagnostics, our technicians restore dependable operation to every top-loading configuration.

High-Efficiency System Repair

LG high-efficiency washer repair addresses the advanced water-saving and energy-efficient technologies built into modern machines. These units use precisely calibrated water levels and specialized wash motions requiring expert calibration. We repair pressure switches measuring water levels, recirculation pumps enhancing cleaning action, and sensors monitoring load size for automatic adjustments. Common high-efficiency issues include inadequate rinsing from incorrect water levels, extended cycle times from sensor malfunctions, and detergent residue buildup requiring specialized cleaning procedures our technicians perform expertly.

Steam Technology Repair

LG steam washer repair covers TurboWash and Allergiene steam cycle technologies that enhance cleaning power and sanitization. We service steam generator assemblies, water injection systems, and temperature sensors controlling steam production. Steam features eliminate allergens, reduce wrinkles, and penetrate fabrics for deeper cleaning. When steam functions fail, we diagnose heating element issues, water supply valve problems, and control board malfunctions. Our technicians restore the premium steam cleaning capabilities that make LG washers exceptional performers.

Smart ThinQ Washer Service

LG ThinQ smart washer repair includes WiFi connectivity diagnostics, smartphone app integration troubleshooting, and smart diagnosis features. Connected washers communicate with matching dryers, receive software updates, and enable remote monitoring. We resolve pairing failures, restore dropped connections, and interpret diagnostic codes transmitted through ThinQ technology. Beyond connectivity, our technicians address the mechanical and electrical issues that smart diagnostics identify, providing comprehensive service for both digital features and physical components.

Common Problems We Solve

Our washer repair expertise covers every symptom homeowners encounter. Won't drain: We clear pump obstructions, replace failed drain pumps, and resolve control board drainage commands. Excessive vibration: We replace worn shock absorbers, balance suspension springs, and ensure proper leveling. Water leaks: We replace door seals, tighten hose connections, and fix tub seal failures. Error codes: We interpret LG diagnostic codes and remedy the underlying failures they indicate.

Gas Dryer Expertise

LG gas dryer repair requires certified expertise in gas combustion systems and safety protocols. Our technicians diagnose igniter failures where components glow but cannot reach temperatures sufficient to open gas valves, gas valve coil deterioration causing intermittent heating, and flame sensor malfunctions providing incorrect readings. We service burner assemblies, gas supply connections, and thermal safety systems. Gas dryer repairs require proper combustion testing and leak verification—our factory-trained specialists ensure safe, efficient operation while maintaining all manufacturer safety standards.

Electric Dryer Solutions

LG electric dryer repair addresses heating element failures, thermal fuse interruptions, and high-limit thermostat malfunctions. Electric dryers require proper 240-volt power supplies—we diagnose outlet problems, failed circuit breakers, and terminal block connection issues. Our technicians replace burned-out heating elements, blown thermal fuses from restricted airflow, and cycling thermostats causing temperature irregularities. We also repair power cord damage, timer malfunctions, and electronic control failures specific to electric configurations, restoring reliable drying performance.

Ventless Dryer Technology

LG ventless dryer repair services condenser-based systems requiring no external exhaust ducting. These sophisticated units use heat exchangers to condense moisture from warm air, collecting water in reservoirs or pumping it to drains. We repair condenser coils requiring periodic cleaning, condensate pumps, water collection systems, and heat exchanger components. Common ventless issues include extended dry times from condenser contamination, tank full errors, and drainage problems. Our technicians understand the closed-loop systems that make ventless technology possible.

Heat Pump Dryer Service

LG heat pump dryer repair covers the most energy-efficient drying technology available, reducing energy consumption up to fifty percent compared to conventional dryers. Heat pump systems recycle warm air using refrigeration principles rather than venting heated air outside. We service compressors, evaporator coils, condenser assemblies, and refrigerant systems. When efficiency declines or error codes appear, our technicians diagnose refrigerant issues, sealed system problems, and the specialized components that make heat pump technology function properly.

Over-the-Range Expertise

LG over-the-range microwave repair services combination units providing both microwave cooking and range ventilation. These installations mount above cooktops, requiring service of ventilation systems alongside microwave components. We repair exhaust fans, grease filters, charcoal filters, and ductwork connections. Over-the-range microwaves feature built-in lighting illuminating cooking surfaces—we replace burned-out bulbs and repair light housings. Our technicians safely service wall-mounted units while protecting surrounding cabinetry, restoring both cooking and ventilation functionality.

Magnetron and Heating

LG microwave heating repair addresses magnetron failures, high-voltage transformer problems, and capacitor malfunctions. The magnetron generates microwave energy—when it fails, food remains cold despite the unit running. We diagnose magnetron output, test high-voltage components, and verify diode functionality. Microwave repairs involve dangerous voltages requiring professional expertise. Our technicians safely discharge capacitors, test magnetron performance, and replace failed components. We restore proper heating power while ensuring safe operation throughout the appliance.

Turntable and Door Systems

LG microwave turntable repair services rotation systems ensuring even cooking. We replace worn turntable motors, damaged roller rings, and cracked glass trays. Door system repair addresses latch failures preventing operation, interlock switches cutting power for safety, and door seal deterioration. Microwaves have multiple safety interlocks that must all function properly—if any interlock fails, the unit won't operate. Our technicians diagnose interlock problems, replace door components, and verify all safety systems function correctly.

Convection and Smart Features

LG convection microwave repair services combination units offering both microwave and convection cooking in single appliances. We repair convection heating elements, fan systems, and combination mode controls. Convection microwaves provide versatility for baking, roasting, and crisping. Smart microwave repair includes ThinQ connectivity, scan-to-cook features reading package barcodes, and Alexa or Google Assistant integration. Our technicians address both smart features and cooking components comprehensively.

Ventilation Service

LG microwave ventilation repair addresses exhaust fans, recirculation systems, and ExtendaVent turbo functions. Over-the-range microwaves must effectively remove cooking smoke, steam, and odors. We service multi-speed fan motors, clean or replace grease-saturated filters, and repair damper assemblies directing airflow. ExtendaVent technology provides extended capture area for improved ventilation—we repair telescoping vent mechanisms and sensors activating automatic ventilation when cooking below generates smoke or heat.
